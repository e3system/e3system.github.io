E3-System: Expressive EmoData, Explicitly Controllable, Easy-to-Use TTS

Anonymous Interspeech submission

Abstract

Recent TTS technology continues to advance, but user demand has raised higher standards for system-level practicality. Existing methods face challenges in balancing expressiveness, controllability, and usability. We propose E3-System, a framework that systematically addresses these challenges.

Our system introduces EmoData, the first thousand-hour multilingual and expressive emotional speech dataset built through a fully automated pipeline. We also propose Dual-TTS, a dual-prompt model trained with a novel strategy that disentangles timbre and style, enabling arbitrary "timbre × style" combinations. Supported by a Style-RAG module, our system significantly reduces user effort while achieving state-of-the-art results in naturalness and style consistency.

1. Expressive EmoData Pipeline

Figure 1: Automated Data Construction Pipeline. To support expressive and multilingual TTS, we construct EmoData, the first thousand-hour multilingual dataset of highly expressive emotional speech. It is constructed via a fully automated and scalable pipeline, covering six emotions. The raw wild speech data undergoes standardization, Voice Activity Detection (VAD) & Source Separation, and Speaker Separation. For emotion labeling, we introduce a confidence-aware ensemble mechanism (Confidence Fusion) to filter samples, ensuring high quality and explicit alignment between semantics and acoustic prosody.

2. Explicitly Controllable Dual-TTS Architecture

Figure 2: The E3-System Model Architecture. The framework covers two core modules: Text-to-Semantic and Semantic-to-Acoustic. The Text-to-Semantic generation utilizes a Dual-AR LLM (Sequential Contextual LLM and Fast Detailed LLM) to process text tokens conditionally with disentangled style and speaker features. A Style-RAG module automatically selects matching style prompts based on captions. The Semantic-to-Acoustic generation employs a Mask-based Diffusion LLM. The model is trained via an innovative VC-based data-driven paradigm to strictly avoid timbre, style, and content leakage.
